# AIRLINE KPI EVALUATION – APRIL 2026

## OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
**Mixed results with concerning operational trends.** While financial metrics show year-over-year revenue growth, operational performance has deteriorated significantly. Customer satisfaction metrics reflect these operational challenges, declining from prior year despite improvements in baggage handling.

---

## METRIC-BY-METRIC ANALYSIS

### OPERATIONAL METRICS

**On-Time Performance: 78.1%** 🔴 **CRITICAL CONCERN**
- Missing target by 6.9 percentage points
- Declined 3.1 points YoY
- *Recommendation:* Conduct root cause analysis on delays (weather, crew, maintenance, turn times). Implement schedule buffer optimization and ground operations task force.

**Load Factor: 86.3%** 🟡 **SLIGHT MISS**
- 1.7 points below target but +1.4 YoY improvement
- Indicates strong demand but potential revenue opportunity loss
- *Recommendation:* Review yield management strategies; the gap suggests pricing may be constraining bookings.

**Cancellation Rate: 2.1%** 🔴 **CONCERN**
- 40% above target (0.6 points)
- Worsening trend (+0.2 YoY)
- *Recommendation:* Immediate review of maintenance reliability and crew availability. Consider schedule rationalization.

### FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue per ASM: 18.4¢** 🟡 **BELOW TARGET**
- Missing target by 0.6¢ (-3.2%)
- Strong YoY growth (+1.2¢, +7.0%)
- *Recommendation:* Pricing power exists but optimize ancillary revenue and premium cabin mix.

**Cost per ASM: 15.1¢** 🔴 **CONCERN**
- 4.1% above target
- Unfavorable 5.6% YoY increase
- *Recommendation:* Urgent cost containment needed. Analyze fuel efficiency, crew productivity, and maintenance costs. Operational issues (delays/cancellations) likely driving incremental costs.

**Total Revenue: $1,420M** 🟡 **UNDERPERFORMANCE**
- $80M shortfall (-5.3% vs target)
- Healthy +8.4% YoY growth
- *Recommendation:* Revenue gap correlates with load factor shortfall; focus on demand capture and network optimization.

### CUSTOMER METRICS

**CSAT: 4.1/5** 🟡 **DECLINING**
- Below target and down 0.2 YoY
- Directly reflects operational issues
- *Recommendation:* Address operational reliability first; implement service recovery protocols.

**NPS: 38** 🔴 **SIGNIFICANT GAP**
- 12 points below target, -6 YoY decline
- Indicates eroding customer loyalty
- *Recommendation:* Critical—link to OTP/cancellations. Launch customer experience recovery initiative.

**Baggage Claim Rate: 3.8/1,000** 🟢 **IMPROVING**
- Above target but 7.3% improvement YoY
- Only metric showing positive operational trend
- *Recommendation:* Continue current baggage handling practices; document best practices for other areas.

---

## TOP 3 CONCERNS

1. **Operational Reliability Crisis** – OTP at 78.1% and cancellation rate at 2.1% are creating a negative spiral affecting customer satisfaction, costs, and revenue
2. **Cost Inflation** – CASM up 5.6% YoY while targets missed, eroding margins despite revenue growth
3. **Customer Loyalty Erosion** – NPS down 6 points YoY signals competitive vulnerability and future revenue risk

---

## PRIORITY RECOMMENDATIONS

### IMMEDIATE (30 days)
- **Operations war room**: Daily reviews of delay/cancellation root causes with cross-functional team
- **Cost control task force**: Target discretionary spending freeze and efficiency initiatives
- **Customer recovery program**: Proactive compensation and communication for affected passengers

### SHORT-TERM (90 days)
- **Schedule optimization**: Right-size network to improve reliability, even if reducing capacity slightly
- **Maintenance & crew staffing review**: Address resource constraints causing operational disruptions
- **Revenue management enhancement**: Close load factor gap through dynamic pricing refinement

### STRATEGIC
- **Invest in operational resilience**: Technology, crew reserves, and spare aircraft to prevent cascading delays
- **Reset performance culture**: Align incentives to reliability metrics across all departments

**Bottom Line:** Revenue growth is positive but unsustainable without addressing operational fundamentals. Prioritize reliability over capacity to rebuild customer trust and control costs.

# AIRLINE OPERATIONS KPI EVALUATION - APRIL 2026

## OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
**Status: MIXED WITH SIGNIFICANT CONCERNS**

Positive trends include year-over-year revenue growth (+8.4%) and improved load factor, indicating strong demand. However, operational execution is deteriorating with declining on-time performance, elevated cancellations, and rising costs creating margin pressure. Customer satisfaction metrics show concerning downward trajectory.

---

## METRIC-BY-METRIC ANALYSIS

### OPERATIONAL METRICS

**On-Time Performance: 78.1%** ⚠️ **CRITICAL**
- Miss target by 6.9 percentage points
- Declined 3.1 points vs. prior year
- **Concern**: Below industry acceptable standards; directly impacts customer satisfaction
- **Recommendation**: Immediate root cause analysis of delays (ground ops, crew scheduling, maintenance). Implement recovery protocols and consider schedule buffer adjustments.

**Load Factor: 86.3%** ✓ **POSITIVE TREND**
- Miss target by 1.7 points but improved +1.4 vs. prior year
- Strong demand signal
- **Recommendation**: Optimize yield management; opportunity exists to reach 88% target through tactical pricing on underperforming routes.

**Cancellation Rate: 2.1%** ⚠️ **CONCERN**
- 40% above target (1.5%)
- Worsening trend (+0.2 vs. prior year)
- **Concern**: Excessive cancellations damage brand and increase costs
- **Recommendation**: Review crew availability, maintenance planning, and aircraft utilization. Consider operational resilience investments.

---

### FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue per ASM: 18.4¢** ✓ **POSITIVE TREND**
- Miss target by 0.6¢ but up 1.2¢ (+7.0%) vs. prior year
- Pricing power evident despite operational challenges
- **Recommendation**: Maintain pricing discipline while monitoring competitive response.

**Cost per ASM: 15.1¢** ⚠️ **CRITICAL**
- 4.1% above target
- Unfavorable increase of 0.8¢ (+5.6%) vs. prior year
- **Concern**: Cost inflation outpacing efficiency gains; margin compression risk
- **Recommendation**: Urgent cost containment review—focus on fuel hedging, crew productivity, and maintenance efficiency. Unit cost trajectory is unsustainable.

**Total Revenue: $1,420M** ⚠️ **BELOW TARGET**
- $80M (5.3%) below target
- Strong +$110M (+8.4%) vs. prior year
- **Recommendation**: Capacity or yield gap analysis needed. Determine if revenue miss is demand-related or execution issue.

---

### CUSTOMER METRICS

**CSAT: 4.1/5** ⚠️ **DECLINING**
- 0.4 points below target
- Down 0.2 vs. prior year
- **Concern**: Correlates with operational failures
- **Recommendation**: Customer experience task force needed; link directly to OTP and cancellation improvement initiatives.

**NPS: 38** ⚠️ **CRITICAL**
- 12 points below target
- Significant decline of 6 points vs. prior year
- **Concern**: Customers are notably less likely to recommend; brand erosion in progress
- **Recommendation**: Executive-level attention required. Survey detractors for specific pain points; likely operational reliability driving dissatisfaction.

**Baggage Claim Rate: 3.8 per 1,000** ✓ **IMPROVING**
- Above target but 7.3% improvement vs. prior year
- **Recommendation**: Continue ground handling improvements; achieve 3.0 target through technology/process enhancements.

---

## TOP 3 CONCERNS

### 1. **OPERATIONAL RELIABILITY CRISIS**
On-time performance (78.1%) and cancellation rate (2.1%) are both deteriorating and significantly below targets. This is the root cause of customer dissatisfaction and likely driving NPS decline.

### 2. **COST STRUCTURE DETERIORATION**
CASM increased 5.6% YoY while only 4.1% above target. With unit revenue growth of 7.0%, margin protection exists today, but the cost trend is unsustainable and threatens profitability.

### 3. **CUSTOMER LOYALTY EROSION**
NPS dropped 6 points YoY to 38—well below the industry "good" threshold of 50. Combined with declining CSAT, this indicates serious brand damage that will impact future revenue and market share.

## SUMMARY
April 2026 reflects an airline with strong demand but failing operational execution. Revenue growth masks underlying problems: reliability issues are damaging customer relationships while costs spiral. **Without immediate operational intervention, the airline risks a negative cycle where poor service drives customer defection, requiring aggressive discounting that further pressures already deteriorating margins.** Leadership must prioritize operational stability as the foundation for financial and customer performance recovery.

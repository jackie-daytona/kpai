# April 2026 KPI Performance Evaluation

## Overall Performance Summary
**Mixed results with concerning operational deterioration.** While revenue shows YoY growth (+8.4%) and load factor improved, operational metrics have declined significantly across all three categories. The airline is underperforming on 8 of 9 KPIs, with operational reliability and customer satisfaction showing the most troubling trends.

---

## Metric-by-Metric Analysis

### OPERATIONAL METRICS

**On-Time Performance: 78.1%** 🔴 CRITICAL
- Miss target by 6.9 percentage points
- Declined 3.1 points YoY
- **Concern**: Below 80% threshold; significant deterioration suggests systemic issues with operations, crew scheduling, or turnaround times

**Load Factor: 86.3%** 🟡 CAUTION
- Miss target by 1.7 points but up 1.4 points YoY
- **Assessment**: Positive YoY trend but still underperforming target; demand is strong but optimization needed

**Cancellation Rate: 2.1%** 🔴 CRITICAL
- 40% above target (1.5%)
- Worsened from 1.9% prior year
- **Concern**: Rising cancellations directly impact revenue and customer trust; likely connected to OTP issues

### FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue per ASM: 18.4¢** 🟡 CAUTION
- 3.2% below target but up 7.0% YoY
- **Assessment**: Strong pricing power and yield improvement, but capacity optimization needed

**Cost per ASM: 15.1¢** 🔴 CONCERN
- 4.1% above target and up 5.6% YoY
- **Concern**: Cost inflation outpacing industry targets; margin compression risk

**Total Revenue: $1,420M** 🟡 CAUTION
- $80M (5.3%) below target but up $110M (8.4%) YoY
- **Assessment**: Good growth trajectory but capacity/schedule optimization could close gap

### CUSTOMER METRICS

**CSAT: 4.1/5** 🔴 CONCERN
- 0.4 points below target; declined 0.2 points YoY
- **Concern**: Negative trend correlates with operational issues; customer experience deteriorating

**NPS: 38** 🔴 CRITICAL
- 12 points below target; dropped 6 points YoY
- **Concern**: Sharp decline in customer advocacy; at risk of losing market share to competitors

**Baggage Claim Rate: 3.8 per 1,000** 🟢 POSITIVE TREND
- Above target but improved 7.3% YoY
- **Assessment**: Only bright spot in customer metrics; continue improvement efforts

---

## Top 3 Concerns

### 1. **Operational Reliability Crisis**
On-time performance and cancellation rates have both deteriorated significantly. This creates a cascading effect: operational disruptions → higher costs → poor customer experience → revenue risk.

### 2. **Customer Satisfaction Collapse**
NPS dropped 6 points YoY (13.6% decline) and CSAT is trending negative. Customers are noticing and reacting to operational problems. Risk of long-term brand damage and competitive vulnerability.

### 3. **Cost Structure Deterioration**
CASM increased 5.6% YoY while missing target by 4.1%. Combined with operational inefficiencies, this threatens profitability despite revenue growth. Current unit margin is only 3.3¢.

---

## Recommendations

### Immediate Actions (0-30 days)
1. **Root cause analysis on OTP and cancellations**: Conduct deep-dive into scheduling, maintenance, crew availability, and ground operations to identify bottlenecks
2. **Revenue recovery task force**: Address $80M revenue shortfall through tactical schedule adjustments and capacity reallocation to higher-performing routes
3. **Cost containment initiative**: Implement hiring freeze on non-critical roles; audit discretionary spending

### Short-term (30-90 days)
4. **Operational recovery plan**: Target 82% OTP and 1.7% cancellation rate by June through improved crew scheduling, maintenance planning, and buffer time in schedules
5. **Customer recovery campaign**: Proactive communication about improvements, enhanced compensation policies for disruptions, loyalty program incentives
6. **Capacity optimization**: Adjust schedule to improve load factor to 87.5%+ while maintaining operational integrity

### Strategic (90+ days)
7. **Operational excellence program**: Invest in predictive maintenance, crew management systems, and operational decision support tools
8. **Cost structure review**: Benchmark CASM against competitors; identify structural cost reduction opportunities (fuel efficiency, labor productivity, vendor negotiations)
9. **Customer experience transformation**: Address pain points revealed in declining CSAT/NPS through service redesign

### Success Metrics (Track Monthly)
- OTP return to >82% by June, >85% by August
- NPS stabilization at 42+ by July
- CASM reduction to 14.8¢ by Q3
- Revenue achievement of 98%+ of target in May-June

**Bottom Line**: The airline is growing revenue but operational execution is undermining profitability and customer loyalty. Without immediate intervention on operations and costs, strong demand cannot be monetized effectively.

# AIRLINE KPI EVALUATION - APRIL 2026

## OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
**Mixed results with significant operational challenges impacting customer experience.** While financial metrics show year-over-year revenue growth and improved load factors, the airline is underperforming across nearly all operational and customer satisfaction targets. Cost pressures and service delivery issues present the most immediate concerns.

---

## METRIC-BY-METRIC ANALYSIS

### OPERATIONAL METRICS

**On-Time Performance: 78.1% ⚠️ CRITICAL**
- 6.9 pts below target | 3.1 pts below prior year
- **Concern**: Declining trend indicates deteriorating operational reliability
- *Recommendation*: Conduct root cause analysis of delays; review turn-time procedures, crew scheduling, and maintenance workflows

**Load Factor: 86.3% ⚡ CAUTION**
- 1.7 pts below target | 1.4 pts above prior year
- **Status**: Positive YoY improvement but missing revenue optimization target
- *Recommendation*: Enhance revenue management strategies; evaluate route-specific performance

**Cancellation Rate: 2.1% ⚠️ CRITICAL**
- 0.6 pts above target | 0.2 pts above prior year
- **Concern**: Worsening trend affecting customer experience and operational costs
- *Recommendation*: Review aircraft maintenance schedules and crew availability; implement better irregular operations recovery protocols

### FINANCIAL METRICS

**Revenue per ASM: 18.4¢ ✓ POSITIVE**
- 0.6¢ below target | 1.2¢ above prior year
- **Status**: Strong YoY growth (+7.0%) despite missing target
- *Recommendation*: Continue pricing optimization; minor gap suggests strategies are working

**Cost per ASM: 15.1¢ ⚠️ CONCERN**
- 0.6¢ above target | 0.8¢ above prior year
- **Concern**: 5.6% cost inflation eroding margins
- *Recommendation*: Urgent cost structure review; identify drivers (fuel, labor, maintenance) and implement efficiency initiatives

**Total Revenue: $1,420M ⚡ CAUTION**
- $80M below target (-5.3%) | $110M above prior year (+8.4%)
- **Status**: Solid growth but capacity/pricing gap exists
- *Recommendation*: Assess if revenue shortfall is demand-driven or capacity-constrained; adjust network strategy accordingly

### CUSTOMER METRICS

**CSAT: 4.1/5 ⚠️ CONCERN**
- 0.4 pts below target | 0.2 pts below prior year
- **Concern**: Declining satisfaction aligns with operational issues
- *Recommendation*: Prioritize service recovery training; address operational root causes

**NPS: 38 🔴 CRITICAL**
- 12 pts below target | 6 pts below prior year
- **Concern**: Significant deterioration in customer advocacy and loyalty
- *Recommendation*: Implement immediate customer feedback loop; connect NPS decline to operational failures

**Baggage Claim Rate: 3.8 per 1,000 ✓ IMPROVING**
- 0.8 above target | 0.3 better than prior year
- **Status**: Positive trend but needs continued focus
- *Recommendation*: Maintain baggage handling improvements; push toward target through technology/process enhancements

---

## TOP 3 CONCERNS

1. **Operational Reliability Breakdown** - On-time performance and cancellation rates have both worsened, creating cascading negative effects on customer satisfaction and operational costs

2. **Customer Loyalty Erosion** - NPS decline of 6 points YoY is severe; poor operational performance is directly damaging brand reputation and repeat business potential

3. **Cost Inflation Pressure** - 5.6% cost increase YoY threatens profitability despite revenue growth; margin compression from 2.9¢ (PY) to 3.3¢ current gap

---

## STRATEGIC RECOMMENDATIONS

### IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (0-30 days)
- **Launch operational recovery task force** to address OTP and cancellation rates through enhanced crew scheduling, maintenance efficiency, and airport operations
- **Implement customer service recovery protocols** including proactive communication during disruptions and compensation guidelines
- **Conduct cost audit** focusing on controllable expenses and vendor contract renegotiation

### SHORT-TERM (1-3 months)
- **Invest in operational technology** (predictive maintenance, better dispatch tools) to improve reliability
- **Realign capacity** on underperforming routes; redeploy to higher-yield markets
- **Enhanced frontline training** focused on service recovery and customer experience during disruptions

### STRATEGIC (3-6 months)
- **Develop operational excellence program** with cross-functional accountability for OTP and completion factor
- **Customer experience transformation** linking operational metrics to NPS through Voice of Customer integration
- **Sustainable cost reduction initiative** targeting 0.6¢+ CASM improvement without compromising safety or service

---

**Bottom Line**: The airline is growing revenue but losing operational discipline and customer trust. Without immediate intervention on reliability metrics, the financial gains will be unsustainable as customers defect and costs spiral from irregular operations.

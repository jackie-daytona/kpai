# AIRLINE KPI EVALUATION – APRIL 2026

## 1. OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY

April 2026 presents a mixed operational picture with significant challenges in core performance areas. While revenue growth of 8.4% YoY demonstrates strong demand recovery and improved load factors, operational execution has deteriorated substantially with on-time performance missing target by 6.9 percentage points and cancellations 40% above acceptable thresholds. Customer satisfaction metrics reflect operational shortfalls, with both CSAT and NPS declining year-over-year despite baggage handling improvements, while cost inflation of 5.6% YoY erodes margin expansion potential.

## 3. TOP 3 CONCERNS

1. **Operational Reliability Collapse Threatens Brand Recovery** – The combination of 78.1% on-time performance (3.1 points below prior year) and 2.1% cancellation rate creates compounding customer disruption. With 21.9% of flights delayed or cancelled, approximately 310,000 passengers experienced service failures this month alone, directly driving the 6-point NPS decline. Impact: Customer lifetime value erosion, increased compensation costs (~$4-6M monthly), and regulatory scrutiny risk.

2. **Cost Inflation Outpacing Revenue Growth Erodes Competitive Position** – CASM increased 5.6% YoY while RASM grew 7.0%, creating only 1.4 percentage point margin expansion versus the 3.0+ point improvement needed to meet annual targets. The 4.1% cost overrun ($6.2M monthly) combined with $80M revenue shortfall creates a $86M monthly performance gap. Impact: Reduced fleet investment capacity, pressure on labor negotiations, and inability to fund customer experience improvements.

3. **Customer Advocacy Metrics Signal Market Share Vulnerability** – NPS of 38 (versus target of 50) places the airline in "detractor zone" where negative word-of-mouth exceeds positive recommendations. With CSAT also declining to 4.1, the airline faces a dual threat of customer defection and reduced pricing power. Industry research indicates each NPS point below 40 correlates to 0.5-1.0% market share risk. Impact: Projected 1-2% revenue headwind in subsequent quarters, estimated $200-400M annual revenue at risk.

## TOP 3 CRITICAL CONCERNS

### 1. **OPERATIONAL RELIABILITY CRISIS**
On-time performance and cancellation rates are both significantly off-target and worsening. This is the root cause driving customer dissatisfaction and likely contributing to cost increases through irregular operations.

### 2. **CUSTOMER LOYALTY EROSION**
NPS at 38 (vs target of 50) represents a 24% shortfall. Combined with declining CSAT, this threatens future revenue and increases customer acquisition costs.

### 3. **COST INFLATION OUTPACING REVENUE GROWTH**
CASM up 5.6% YoY while RASM up 7.0% provides only thin margin protection. With operational issues limiting capacity utilization, profitability is at risk.

## RECOMMENDATIONS

### IMMEDIATE ACTIONS (30 days)
1. **Launch operational reliability task force** - Conduct root cause analysis of OTP and cancellations. Focus on: maintenance turn times, crew scheduling, airport ground operations, and weather contingency planning.

2. **Implement irregular operations (IROPS) cost control** - Quantify the financial impact of delays/cancellations. Prioritize investments that improve completion factor.

3. **Customer service recovery protocol** - Deploy proactive compensation/communication for affected passengers to stop NPS bleeding.

### SHORT-TERM INITIATIVES (90 days)
4. **Network optimization review** - Evaluate if schedule is operationally achievable with current resources. Consider reducing frequencies on problematic routes to improve reliability.

5. **Cost management deep-dive** - Identify drivers of CASM increase. Likely culprits: fuel hedging, IROPS costs, crew overtime, and maintenance delays.

6. **Competitive benchmarking** - Compare OTP and customer metrics against competitors to assess relative market position.

### STRATEGIC FOCUS
7. **Reliability over capacity** - Accept temporary load factor/revenue trade-offs to rebuild operational performance and customer trust. Poor reliability is more expensive long-term than underutilized capacity.

## BOTTOM LINE
**The airline is experiencing an operational performance crisis that's eroding customer loyalty and inflating costs.** While revenue shows YoY growth, this is masking fundamental operational problems. Without immediate intervention to improve reliability, expect accelerating customer defection and margin compression in coming months. **Priority: Fix operations first—revenue recovery will follow.**

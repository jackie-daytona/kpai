# Airline Operations KPI Analysis - April 2026

## OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
**Mixed results with significant operational concerns.** Financial metrics show positive YoY growth but miss targets. Operational performance has deteriorated across all key metrics, driving customer satisfaction declines. The airline is growing revenue but struggling with execution quality.

## TOP 3 CONCERNS

### 1. **OPERATIONAL RELIABILITY CRISIS** 🔴
The combination of poor OTP (78.1%), elevated cancellations (2.1%), and declining customer metrics indicates a **systemic operational breakdown**. This is the root cause driving customer dissatisfaction.

### 2. **CUSTOMER LOYALTY EROSION** 🔴
NPS declining from 44 to 38 (-6 points) with CSAT also falling represents **significant brand damage**. Customers are experiencing unreliable service and losing trust, threatening long-term revenue.

### 3. **COST ESCALATION SQUEEZING MARGINS** 🔴
CASM increased 5.6% YoY while RASM grew 7.0%, but the **margin compression risk is real**. At 15.1¢ vs 18.4¢, operating margin is only ~18% before overhead, and operational inefficiencies (delays, cancellations) are likely driving excess costs.

---

## KEY RECOMMENDATIONS

### Immediate Actions (0-30 days)
1. **Establish Operations War Room**: Daily cross-functional meetings to address OTP and cancellations
2. **Service Recovery Blitz**: Increase compensation/goodwill budget, deploy customer care teams to high-impact stations
3. **Cost Control Audit**: Identify and eliminate operational waste, particularly delay-related expenses

### Short-term Initiatives (1-3 months)
4. **Crew and Maintenance Review**: Ensure adequate staffing and reduce aircraft out-of-service time
5. **Schedule Optimization**: Build in additional buffer time at congested airports; reduce vulnerable connections
6. **Enhanced Customer Communication**: Proactive notifications, realistic ETAs, transparent rebooking processes

### Strategic Focus (3-6 months)
7. **Technology Investment**: Predictive maintenance, integrated operations control, baggage tracking
8. **Capacity-Demand Rebalancing**: Align network growth with operational capability—quality over quantity
9. **Employee Engagement**: Operational frontline staff morale likely impacted; invest in training and support

---

## BOTTOM LINE
**The airline is experiencing growing pains.** Revenue growth is healthy (+8.4%), but operational execution cannot support current capacity levels, resulting in poor customer experience and cost inefficiencies. **Prioritize operational stability over growth** in the near term—restoring reliability will protect brand equity and improve unit economics. Without intervention, the current trajectory risks a negative spiral of customer defection and margin erosion.

# AIRLINE KPI EVALUATION - APRIL 2026

## OVERALL PERFORMANCE SUMMARY
**Mixed results with concerning operational deterioration.** While revenue metrics show YoY growth, operational performance has declined significantly across all key metrics, directly impacting customer satisfaction. The airline is underperforming against targets in 8 of 9 KPIs.
